Welcome To Kisan Network

Kisan Network is a Y-Combinator and Thiel Fellowship backed online marketplace for the $200B+ Indian agricultural ecosystem. We empower small and marginal farmersby enabling them to sell their produce directly to businesses using their smartphone.
